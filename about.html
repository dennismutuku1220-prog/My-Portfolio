Who I Am

I am Dennis Mutuku Munyao, a dedicated student pursuing a diploma in Computer Science while currently serving as an attaché at Makueni County Government (September-December 2025). My journey combines academic excellence in technology with practical experience in public service, driven by a commitment to innovation and meaningful contributions to society.

My Background

Growing up in Kenya, I developed a strong passion for technology and community development. This foundation led me to pursue a diploma in Computer Science while simultaneously gaining valuable experience in government service. My unique combination of technical education and public administration experience positions me to bridge the gap between technology and public service.

Current Role

I am currently serving as an attaché at Makueni County Government (September-December 2025), where I am gaining valuable hands-on experience in supporting various administrative functions while applying my computer science knowledge to improve digital processes and contribute to the efficient delivery of government services. This 4-month attachment is providing me with invaluable experience in public administration, stakeholder management, and the practical application of technology in government operations.
